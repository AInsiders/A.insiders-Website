üìö How Steganography Works

Steganography is the practice of concealing information within other non-secret data or a physical object. In this tool, we use the least significant bits (LSB) of image pixels to store encrypted data, making the changes virtually undetectable to the human eye.

üîê AES-256 Encryption

AES-256 is a symmetric encryption algorithm that uses a 256-bit key to encrypt and decrypt data. It's considered one of the most secure encryption standards available and is used by governments and organizations worldwide for protecting sensitive information.
